Case study

A wealth-planning platform that replaced a stack of spreadsheets.

We designed and built a client-facing planning platform for a wealth-advisory firm — turning brittle, hand-built models into auditable software clients can log into.

Sector
Wealth management
Type
Client-facing SaaS
Stack
React · Supabase · Stripe · Plaid
Scope
Product design + full build

The problem

Advisors modeled each client's financial future in one-off spreadsheets. Every projection was rebuilt by hand, hard to audit, and impossible to brand and hand to a client. The firm's best thinking was trapped in cells only its authors could read.

What we built

A planning portal with a real projection engine underneath it, plus the modeling tools advisors actually reach for:

  • A projection engine using current-year tax brackets, with estate-tax modeling and cash-flow forecasting
  • Retirement-specific logic: Roth five-year rule, Social Security spousal timing, and tax-loss carryforward
  • Monte Carlo simulation for probability-of-success, not just a single straight-line forecast
  • A saved-scenario system so advisors compare paths side by side
  • Full Excel round-trip — existing models import and export cleanly, with the math intact
  • Bank and brokerage account linking to pull real balances in
  • White-label theming and per-advisor activity tracking
The outcome

One platform that replaces the spreadsheet stack. Advisors model, save scenarios, and share a branded portal with clients — and the math is auditable on screen instead of buried in a workbook.
