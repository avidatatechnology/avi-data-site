About

A founder-led data studio.

Avi Data Technologies isn't a faceless agency that hands you a deck and disappears. The person who scopes your data is the person who builds the dashboard — and the one who picks up the phone when a number looks off.

We started where most reporting actually lives: a folder of exports, a dozen tabs, and one analyst who knows which file is the "real" one. Our job is to take that fragile, hand-kept system and turn it into something the whole team can trust and log into — without asking anyone to change tools or learn a new platform.

We've built this across auto retail, staffing, distribution, and wealth — anywhere a team was running on spreadsheets that had outgrown them.
