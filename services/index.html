What we build

Most of our work falls into a handful of shapes. Engagements usually combine a few of them.

Custom dashboards

One screen for the numbers your team checks daily, pulled live from the systems you already use.

  • Excel, CSV & CRM exports unified
  • Performance, pipeline & coverage views
  • Role-based access per team

Financial models & projection tools

Projection engines, scenario modeling, and reporting that replace fragile, one-off spreadsheets.

  • Multi-year projections & scenarios
  • Tax, estate & cash-flow modeling
  • Monte Carlo & sensitivity analysis

Internal business apps

Tools your team uses to do the work, not just watch it — trackers, queues, and admin portals.

  • Workflow & ticket trackers
  • Approval & request queues
  • Admin portals with audit logs

Data integration & automation

Connect the systems you already pay for and kill the manual export-and-paste cycle for good.

  • Nightly jobs & scheduled syncs
  • Realtime updates across users
  • Stripe, banking & API connections

Client portals & billing

Branded portals where your customers log in, see their data, and pay — without the back-and-forth.

  • Invoicing with ACH & card
  • Recurring & maintenance billing
  • Document sharing & access control

Ongoing care

We don't disappear at launch. Software you depend on gets monitoring, support, and steady iteration.

  • Care plans & SLAs
  • Error logging & monitoring
  • New views as your needs change
